Napoleon News Agency ("Napoleon News", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to maintaining a high standard of editorial integrity and transparency in all forms of content, including advertising and sponsorships.

This Advertising Policy outlines the principles that govern advertising placements, sponsored content, partnerships, and promotional materials displayed on [https://napoleonnews.com](https://napoleonnews.com) (the "Site") and all associated platforms (including newsletters, events, and social media).

#### 1. General Principles

We accept advertising that aligns with our values and complies with applicable laws and industry standards. We reserve the right to refuse, reject, or cancel any advertisement at our sole discretion, for any reason, including but not limited to content that we deem misleading, offensive, inappropriate, or harmful to our reputation or users.

#### 2. Advertising Formats Covered

This policy applies to all forms of advertising across Napoleon News channels, including but not limited to:

a. Display/banner ads
b. Native and sponsored content
c. Newsletter advertisements
d. Event sponsorships
e. Social media collaborations
f. Branded video or audio content
g. Advertorials
h. Classified ads or press releases submitted for a fee

#### 3. Sponsored and Native Content

Sponsored or branded content must:

a. Be clearly labeled as "Sponsored," "Paid Partnership," "Promoted," "In Partnership With," or a similar designation
b. Be distinguishable from editorial content and not interfere with or influence our independent reporting
c. Meet our editorial, factual, and ethical standards

We maintain full editorial control over any sponsored content published in our voice or style. Sponsored content must comply with all applicable advertising laws and regulations.

#### 4. Editorial Independence

Napoleon News maintains a strict separation between advertising and editorial content. Advertisers do not have influence over our reporting, investigations, headlines, reviews, or editorial decisions. Acceptance of an advertisement does not constitute an endorsement of the advertiser, its products, or its services.

Our journalists and editorial staff do not accept gifts, payment, or benefits from advertisers.

#### 5. Prohibited Content

We do not accept advertising that contains, promotes, or links to:

a. False, misleading, or unsubstantiated claims
b. Hate speech or content that discriminates based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or age
c. Pornographic or sexually explicit material
d. Alcohol, tobacco, recreational drugs, or weapons (unless relevant in a responsible journalistic context)
e. Malware, spyware, or disruptive software
f. Fake news or clickbait
g. Political campaigns or messages without disclosure and vetting

We also reject ads from competitors who misrepresent Napoleon News or attempt to exploit our branding.

#### 6. Technical and Design Requirements

All advertising must:

a. Load securely (HTTPS)
b. Not interfere with page performance, user experience, or accessibility
c. Avoid auto-playing audio or video unless muted by default
d. Be responsive across devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
e. Be free of viruses, tracking without consent, or misleading redirects 

#### 7. Data and Privacy Compliance

Advertisers must comply with all relevant data protection laws including:

a. Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA)
b. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if targeting EU users
c. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
d. Any other relevant law under any given jurisdiction

Advertisers may not collect personally identifiable information through Napoleon News without our knowledge and user consent. Use of cookies or third-party tracking pixels must comply with our Cookie and Privacy Policies.

#### 8. Political Advertising

Political ads are only accepted with:

a. Proper authorization and disclosure
b. Full transparency on the identity of sponsors or candidate representatives
c. Compliance with national election regulations

We reserve the right to flag, label, or reject political content that may misinform the public or violate our standards.

#### 9. User Feedback and Complaints

If users believe an ad on our platform is misleading, inappropriate, or harmful, they may report it to:
[adverts@napoleonnews.com](mailto:adverts@napoleonnews.com) or [info@napoleonnews.com](mailto:info@napoleonnews.com)
We investigate and act promptly.

#### 10. Updates to This Policy

This Advertising Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our advertising practices, regulatory changes, or company policies. Advertisers are encouraged to review the policy regularly.
